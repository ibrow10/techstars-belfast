Belfast's startup ecosystem is entering a new chapter. In partnership with Queen's University and Ormeau Labs, we are launching Techstars in Northern Ireland—providing local founders with a direct line to a global network of mentors, investors, and resources.


Techstars Belfast is a founder-led startup community for Northern Ireland, powered by the global Techstars network. It exists to help ambitious founders start, build, and connect — regardless of stage — by providing access to world-class networks, practical learning, and high-quality peer support.

HOW WE HELP FOUNDERS

1. Founder Community & Network

At its core, Techstars Belfast is a community of ambitious founders, operators, and builders. It connects you into the global Techstars ecosystem — thousands of founders, mentors, and investors worldwide — while remaining deeply rooted in Northern Ireland.

2. Pre-Accelerator & Early-Stage Programmes

Structured, time-bound programmes designed for founders at the very earliest stages. These programmes help you move from idea to validated problem, early product, and a credible path to funding. The focus is on execution, clarity, and founder confidence.

3. Hackathons & Build Sprints

Regular hackathons and build sprints give founders space to experiment, prototype, and ship quickly. Practical, hands-on events designed to compress weeks of work into days, often in partnership with leading global tech companies.

4. Founder Education & Masterclasses

Targeted masterclasses covering the fundamentals of building a startup: customer discovery, go-to-market, fundraising readiness, AI-native product design, and founder storytelling. Led by experienced founders and investors.

5. Events, Meetups & Founder Dinners

A steady cadence of informal events creating space for honest conversations and peer learning. Designed to reduce founder isolation and help you learn from people a few steps ahead of you.

6. Partner-Led Opportunities & Perks

Access to software credits, tools, early product access, and real commercial exposure through partnerships with global technology companies. Practical and founder-first, focused on building faster and smarter.

7. Pathways to Capital & Next-Stage Support

While we don't invest directly, we provide warm pathways to capital and later-stage programmes. The emphasis is on readiness and fit — making sure founders raise money at the right time, from the right people.

IN SHORT

Techstars Belfast is a launchpad, not an accelerator. It’s where founders come to find community, build momentum, learn the fundamentals, and connect into a global network — without giving up equity or being forced into a one-size-fits-all programme.
