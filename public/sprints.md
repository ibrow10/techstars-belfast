# Techstars Belfast Sprints

The AI-era evolution of Startup Weekend: ship in hours or days, not weeks. A sprint is a focused build session where founders, operators and investors are in the same room shipping working prototypes against real challenges.

## Recent and example sprints

- **SVV Build Sprint (Belfast, with Sure Valley Ventures)**: a focused build session with the SVV team and their portfolio, shipping working prototypes against real challenges.
- **AI Build Day (The AMP Growth Incubator, Derry, with Invest NI)**: a day-long AI build event; morning intro to models and tooling, a two-hour afternoon build sprint on workflows, websites, agents or products, ending in a show-and-tell.
- **HackBelfast 2026 (Queen's University Belfast)**: community partner for Northern Ireland's largest student-led hackathon; 175+ students, 24 hours, real-world challenges.

## Who sprints are open to

Universities and research groups, funds and investors, and partner organisations who want founders building against real problems. Upcoming sprint dates appear on the [events page](https://techstarsbelfast.com/events.md).
