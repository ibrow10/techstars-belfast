# Techstars Founder Catalyst (via Techstars Belfast)

Build a fundable company from Belfast, with a global Techstars cohort.

Founder Catalyst is a 10-week, part-time, virtual pre-accelerator delivered through Techstars Belfast, a formal partnership between Ormeau Labs, Queen's University Belfast and Techstars. No equity is taken. If you are building something ambitious in Northern Ireland, it is the fastest credible on-ramp into the global Techstars ecosystem.

Applications for the Fall 2026 programme: https://www.techstars.com/techstars-founder-catalyst-global-fall-2026-program

## What you get

- Weekly live masterclasses taught by Techstars leaders and experts
- Bonus workshops on practical founder execution
- 1:1 guidance and support from Techstars mentors, operators and investors
- Weekly pitch coaching to sharpen your investor story
- Exclusive perks (tools, credits, support)
- A global peer cohort for accountability and collaboration

## How the programme works

1. **Masterclass track** (core founder build): customer discovery to ICP to PMF evidence; GTM wedge and experiments; metrics and traction narrative; financial model and runway logic; pitch and fundraising readiness.
2. **Pitch feedback drop-ins** (repetition loop): pitch, get direct critique, iterate, pitch again.
3. **Partner workshops** (operator-level help): examples from the Belfast track include sales pipeline management using AI (HubSpot), AI infrastructure architecture (AWS), investor pipeline management (Metal).
4. **Community track** (pattern recognition and ambition): fundraising fundamentals, unicorn journeys and founder mental models, what great investors listen for.

## Curriculum highlights

- The Customer Playbook: discovery to PMF
- Forecasting for success with financial modelling
- When to scale and how fast
- Go-to-market foundations: picking the right first channel
- From story to stage: mastering the winning pitch
- Product: roadmapping, testing, prioritising and launching
- The magic of metrics

## What you leave with

A clear ICP and problem statement backed by real customer conversations; a tested GTM wedge; a simple, defensible financial model; a hypothesis-driven product roadmap; a metrics view that makes sense; a tight 3-4 minute pitch matched to evidence; and a more professional investor pipeline approach if fundraising is the right next step.

## Eligibility

- Beyond ideation (moving toward product or growth)
- Pre- or post-revenue; if post-revenue, MRR up to $5k
- Raised no more than $400k external funding
- Traction signals encouraged (pilots, users, waitlist, pre-orders)
- Offered exclusively to founders from partner communities, including Belfast

## FAQ

**Is this in person?** It is a virtual, part-time programme running across 10 weeks.

**Do you take equity?** No. There is no equity exchange in Founder Catalyst programmes.

**I'm a researcher without a fully formed startup. Can I apply?** If you are beyond pure ideation and can show real problem insight plus evidence gathering (discovery, pilots, waitlist, early users), you are in the zone Techstars looks for.

**Why trust this?** Delivered through Techstars Belfast, a formal partnership between Ormeau Labs, Queen's and Techstars. Techstars has supported 10,000+ companies and founders globally.
