# About Techstars Belfast

Techstars Belfast is a founder community and pre-accelerator pathway in Northern Ireland: a formal Community Partnership between Ormeau Labs, Queen's University Belfast and Techstars. Backed by partners including Coinbase, Stripe and ElevenLabs.

## The stack

- **Events and founder nights**: meet peers, learn fast, build momentum
- **Sprints**: our AI-era evolution of Startup Weekend; ship in hours or days, not weeks
- **Founder Catalyst**: a structured pre-accelerator pathway delivered through Techstars ([detail](https://techstarsbelfast.com/catalyst.md))
- **Community Development Lab**: coordinated ecosystem action, with founders in the room first

## Why now

AI is changing how products are built (speed, tooling, smaller teams), which markets are accessible (global from day one), and which jobs and industries get disrupted (workflow-heavy sectors in particular). Northern Ireland needs founders who try building companies earlier, meet collaborators faster, access world-class mentorship and peer networks, and develop global ambition without leaving home.

## Principles

- Founders first: practical help over theatre
- Ship fast: working demos beat slides
- AI-native by default: the future is already here
- Global ambition, local roots: build from here, ship everywhere
